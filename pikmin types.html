Red Pikmin

The red pikmin is the first pikmin type you encounter in the game, it is resistant to fire and thanks to its pointy nose, it can do more damage than the average pikmin !

Yellow Pikmin

The yellow pikmin has big ears and a lighter body than the rest of the pikmins, thanks to that it can be thrown further, it is resistant to electricity and can dig faster than the average pikmin.

Blue Pikmin

The blue pikmin has gills on his cheeks and a little mouth, it can breath underwater and is the only pikmin type that can do that

Purple Pikmin

The purple pikmin is the heaviest pikmin type, being 10 times the weight of an average pikmin, it can also carry items that would normally ask 10 pikmins, he can also stun an enemy when thrown.

White Pikmin

The white pikmin is the fastest pikmin type, it is resistant to poison and if an enemy eats it, the enemy takes poison damage

Rock Pikmin

The rock pikmin is a solid fellow, its body being hard as a rock, he cannot get crushed by enemies and can break throught glass

Winged Pikmin

The winged pikmin can fly, it can carry items overland and can take shortcuts when doing so, they aren't the best fighters...

Ice Pikmin

The ice pikmin is capable of freezing enemies when a bunch of them are thrown at once, they can also freeze water and float over it, if an enemy eats them, it gets frozen too !

Glow Pikmin

The glow pikmin is only appearing at night or in caves, they can resist pretty much all of the effects in the game, and when multiple are aquired, they can form a spirit bomb just like in dragon ball !
